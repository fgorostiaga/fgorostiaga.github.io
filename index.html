Felipe Gorostiaga

About me

I obtained a degree in Computer Science at the Universidad Nacional de Rosario, Argentina, and a Master in Software and Systems at the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (UPM), Spain. I completed my PhD at the IMDEA Software Institute, Spain, under the supervision of César Sánchez, and continued there as a postdoctoral researcher. I am currently the Technical Lead of the development team at an AI-based agricultural company.

Research Interests

I'm interested in Formal Methods and Software Engineering in general, and in particular, Runtime Verification and Reactive Synthesis.
