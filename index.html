About me

I'm a Ph.D. student at the Madrid Institute for Advanced Studies in Software Development Technologies (IMDEA Software Institute), Spain. I work under the supervision of César Sánchez. I obtained a Master in Software and Systems at the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (UPM), Spain. I previously obtained a degree in Computer Science at the Universidad Nacional de Rosario, Argentina.

Research Interests

I'm interested in Formal Methods and Software Engineering in general, and in particular:

  • Formal Program Verification
  • Runtime Verification
  • Stream Runtime Verification
